Kinso Recipe App

From my interpretation of the brief, I was drawn to problems that users may have getting “new” ingredients for recipes that they wish to try out. It’s not un-common for users to run out of ingredients when trying to cook something new. Alternatives are often sought which may defeat the purpose of trying a new recipe in the first place.

In approaching this responsive web App I explored issues relating to how users search for and approach preparing meals. This includes the absence of relevant ingredient when users want to cook something new and problems, they have trying to relate this to shopping habits.
